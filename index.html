This portal provides gene expression and eQTL data for different rat tissues. It is managed by the NIDA center for genetic studies of drug abuse in outbred rats (P50DA037844). Currently it includes data for eye and five brain regions from Heterogeneous Stock rats: infralimbic cortex (IL), prelimbic cortex (PL), orbitofrontal cortex (OFC), nucleus accumbens core (NAcc), and lateral habenula (LHb).
